Memory Drive · 🏁

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Memory Drive is a memory card-matching game by Lumi. Short version: we collect as little as possible, and nothing that identifies you personally.

1. Data you provide

Only a self-chosen nickname and avatar emoji. Please don't use your real full name. We never ask for email, phone, real name, address or photos.

2. On your device

Your progress (levels, stars, coins, settings, achievements, purchases) is stored locally on your device. We cannot see it. Delete the app or clear data to remove it.

3. Online features

Online duels, daily challenges and leaderboards send only your nickname, avatar and game results to our server (Cloudflare Workers). Duel rooms auto-delete within 15 minutes. If you never play online, nothing is sent.

4. Purchases

In-app purchases (coins, PRO) are handled entirely by Apple. We never see or store your payment details.

5. What we do NOT do

No ads, no trackers, no analytics, no fingerprinting, no selling/sharing data, no accounts or passwords, no access to contacts, photos, location or microphone.

6. Children

Suitable for all ages. Online nicknames are visible to opponents, so advise children to use a made-up name.

7. Deletion & contact

Local data: delete the app. Leaderboard entry: email us your nickname and we'll delete it. Contact: hellostorelumi@gmail.com.

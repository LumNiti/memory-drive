🏁 Memory Drive — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Memory Drive is a memory card-matching game developed by Lumi ("we", "us"). This policy explains what data the game handles. Short version: we collect as little as possible, and nothing that identifies you personally.

1. Data you provide

When you create your in-game profile, you choose a display name (nickname) and an avatar (emoji). These are the only "personal" data in the game. Please do not use your real full name — any nickname works. We never ask for your email, phone number, real name, address, or photos.

2. Data stored on your device

Your game progress (levels, stars, coins, settings, achievements, purchases) is stored locally on your device (browser localStorage or app storage). We cannot see it. Deleting the app or clearing browser data removes it.

3. Data sent to our server (online features only)

When you use online duels, daily challenges, or leaderboards, the game sends to our server (Cloudflare Workers): your chosen nickname, avatar emoji, and game results (times, scores, wins). Duel room data auto-deletes within 15 minutes. Leaderboard entries contain only your nickname, avatar, and score. If you never use online features, nothing is sent.

4. Purchases

In-app purchases (coins, PRO) are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store / Apple Pay. We never see or store your payment details. Purchases are subject to Apple's own privacy policy.

5. What we do NOT do

  • No advertising, no ad networks, no trackers
  • No analytics SDKs, no fingerprinting
  • No selling or sharing of data with third parties
  • No account creation, no passwords, no emails
  • No access to your contacts, photos, location, or microphone

6. Children

The game is suitable for all ages. Because we collect no personal information beyond a self-chosen nickname and emoji, no special parental data concerns apply. Parents: online duel nicknames are visible to opponents, so advise children to use a made-up name.

7. Data deletion

Local data: delete the app or clear browser data. Server data: duel rooms expire automatically; to remove a leaderboard entry, contact us with your nickname and we will delete it.

8. Contact

Questions or deletion requests: hellostorelumi@gmail.com

9. Changes

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change. Significant changes will be noted in the game.
